四级核心500词

abruptly adv. 突然地

相关单词/词组：

interrupt v. 打断

bankrupt adj. 破产的

erupt v. 爆发，喷发

The speech abruptly closed. 演讲突然结束。

He turned abruptly and walked away. 他突然转身走开了。

absorb v. 吸收

相关单词/词组：

absorption n. 吸收

Plants absorb oxygen. 植物吸收氧气。

Black walls absorb a lot of heat during the day. 黑色墙壁在白天吸收大量的热。

abuse v. n. 滥用，虐待

相关单词/词组：

abnormal adj. 反常的，变态的

child abuse 虐待儿童

abuse authority 滥用职权

The system is open to abuse. 这项制度容易被滥用。

Many children suffer racial abuse at school. 许多孩子在学校遭受种族虐待。

academic adj. 学术的，学院的

相关单词/词组：

academy n. 学院

He is unsuited to academic work. 他不适合做学术工作。

It is a purely academic question. 这是一个纯理论问题。

access v. 获取 n. 接近，入口

相关单词/词组：

process n. 过程

accessible adj. 易接近的

Cats should always have access to fresh, clean water. 应该让猫总能喝到新鲜、干净的水。

Does the hotel have wheelchair access ? 这家旅馆有轮椅通道吗？

accommodate v. 适应，提供住宿

相关单词/词组：

mode n. 模式

Her eyes took a while to accommodate to the darkness. 她的眼睛过了一会才适应黑暗。

The island was used to accommodate child refugees. 这个岛用来收容儿童难民。

accomplish v. 实现

相关单词/词组：

achieve v. 完成，实现

If we’d all work together, i think we could accomplish our goal. 如果我们齐心协力，我想我们能实现我们的目标。

The task is accomplished by great effort. 这项任务是经过巨大努力才完成的。

account for 解释，占…比例

相关单词/词组：

explain v. 解释

accounting n. 会计

discount n. 折扣

Can you account for your movements on that night ? 你能解释一下你那天晚上的行踪吗？

Afro-Americans account for 12% of the US population. 非裔美国人占美国人口的12%。

accumulate v. 积累，增加

Fat tends to accumulate around the hips and thighs. 脂肪常常积聚在臀部和大腿部位。

It was his ambition to accumulate a million dollars before he turned thirty. 他的雄心是在30岁之前积累100万美元。

accurate adj. 准确的，精确的

相关单词/词组：

cure n. 治疗

Police believe Derek gave a reasonably accurate account of what happened. 警方相信德里克对事情的叙述是比较准确的。

The evidence she gave to the court was not strictly accurate. 她向法庭提供的证词并不完全准确。

achieve v. 达到，实现

相关单词/词组：

achievement n. 成就

accomplish v. 实现

She eventally achieved her goal of becoming a professor. 她终于实现目标，当上了教授。

Frances achieved very good exam results. 弗朗西斯考试取得了优异的成绩。

acknowledge v. 承认

He acknowledges that when he’s tired he gets bad tempered. 他承认自己累了就会发脾气。

Many of the poor acknowledged him as their spirtual leader. 许多穷人都把他视为他们的精神领袖。

acquire v. 得到

相关单词/词组：

require v. 要求

The college acquired a reputation for very high standards. 那所学院以学术水平而闻名。

She has acquired and an email address and a site on the World Wide Web. 她获得了一个电子邮件地址和万维网网址。

acute adj. 敏锐的

相关单词/词组：

cute adj. 可爱的，聪明的

Young children have a particularly acute sense of smell. 幼儿的嗅觉特别灵敏。

Simon’s vague manner concealed an acute mind. 西蒙貌似蒙昧，其实头脑灵活。

adapt v.适应于

相关单词/词组：

adopt v. 收养，采用

The children are finding it hard to adapt to the new school. 孩子们觉得很难适应新学校。

How do these insects adapt themselves to new environments ? 这些昆虫是如何适应新环境的？

addiction n. 上瘾，沉溺

相关单词/词组：

be addicted to … 对…上瘾，沉溺于…

predict v. 预言

diction n. 用语，措词

He has never conquered his addiction to smoking. 他从未戒除过烟瘾。

She helped him fight his drug addiction. 她帮助她对抗了自己的毒瘾。

additional adj. 额外的

Additional information can be obtained from the centre. 可以从中心获得更多的信息。

The filming imposed an additional strain on her. 影片拍摄使她承受的额外的压力。

address v. 解决，处理，发表演讲

The new president delivered his inaugural address in Creole. 新总统用克里奥尔语发表就职演说。

Our products address the nees of real users. 我们的产品满足真正用户的需要。

adequate adj. 足够的，充分的

相关单词：

equation n. 等式

Farmers have been slow to make adequate provision for their retirement. 农民迟迟没有为退休做好充分的准备。

She didn’t give an adequate explanation for being late. 她没有给出充分的理由说明迟到的原因。

administration n. 管理，政府

相关单词：

minister n. 部长，大臣

administer v. 管理

administrative adj. 管理的

We’re looking for someone with experience in administration. 我们正在寻找一个有管理经验的人。

I work in the sales administration department. 我在销售管理部门工作。
